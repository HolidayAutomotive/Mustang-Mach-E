SEAMLESS TECHNOLOGY

The Mustang Mach-E features connected tech designed to provide a driving experience that’s centered around you.

Drive Experiences

Your Mustang Mach-E will match your mood. You can select your Drive Experience to fit the moment—and the interior sound, lighting, and even the responsiveness will adjust to your style – whether it’s Unbridled, Engage or, Whisper.

Introducing SYNC® 4A

This unique, human-centered design, displayed on a 15.5-inch touchscreen, combines cloud-based connectivity, over-the-air update capabilities, voice recognition and so much more to bring you a seamless and personalized driving
experience.

Over-The-Air Updates

As technology evolves, your Mustang Mach-E evolves with it. Secure over-the-air updates offer enhancements throughout your SUV, from the SYNC® 4A communications system to upgrades that help improve your vehicle’s quality, capability and convenience over time. Many of these updates will be virtually invisible, enabled by an innovative cloud-connected platform.

CONNECT AND CONTROL

Connect your Mustang Mach-E to the FordPassTM App and unlock powerful ownership experiences. Make your drive your own: map favorite routes, set a preferred charging station and track your service. FordPass can even develop a custom charging schedule based on your habits.

Driver Profile and Personalization

By connecting through FordPass, your Mustang Mach-E recognizes your phone as soon as you get close. Then it automatically adjusts the seats, mirror, radio, ambient lighting and instrument panel just the way you like it. You can personalize it even further by creating your own unique avatar.

Phone as a Key

No more fumbling with your fob. Use your phone as a key to start and operate your Mustang Mach-E. With the FordPass App, your phone can roll your windows up and down, activate the panic alarm and welcome mode, open your lift gate and engage the remote start feature in your Mustang Mach-E without the need for a physical key fob.

Trip Planner

FordPass Power My Trip makes trip planning hassle-free. Get behind the wheel and we’ll tailor a route to your destination based on your battery level and available charging stations. Goodbye range anxiety.

Remote Vehicle Controls

Lock/unlock, start/stop, monitor charging and create a start schedule from virtually anywhere with complimentary remote vehicle controls. When equipped with FordPass ConnectTM
